02 — Method

How a hosted journey gets built.

Every Dream Travel trip is shaped to a host and their people. Four quiet stages, no template, no rush.

  1. 01

    Listen first.

    We start with a real conversation. Who travels with you, how they like to spend a long afternoon, what your group has loved before, what it never wants to repeat.

  2. 02

    Shape the trip.

    A custom itinerary is drafted around your pace and theme. Wine and table; art and quiet streets; coastline and slow mornings. We choose suppliers we have worked with in person.

  3. 03

    Carry the logistics.

    Bookings, transfers, dietary notes, supplier coordination, the small things that turn a good idea into an easy trip to join. Your group sees one clean document. We carry the rest.

  4. 04

    Travel beside you.

    A point of contact through the whole trip, available before the first plane and through the last dinner. So you can host your people and not the logistics.

03 — Journeys

A few of the trips we keep shaping.

Not destinations, exactly. Closer to a way of being in a place for a week.

  • 01

    The Lisbon Table

    Lisbon · Sintra · Alentejo  /  7 nights  /  10 — 16 guests

    Tile-lined courtyards, family wineries, long afternoons at tables that were set hours before you arrived. Built for groups who want depth over distance.

  • 02

    Puglia Slow

    Bari · Ostuni · Lecce  /  8 nights  /  8 — 14 guests

    Olive country at the pace of olive country. Masseria stays, a private cooking afternoon, evenings in stone towns where nothing closes early.

  • 03

    Provence Harvest

    Avignon · L’Isle-sur-la-Sorgue · Gordes  /  6 nights  /  10 — 18 guests

    Late-summer fields, market mornings, a small private cellar in the hills. A trip you plan once and your group talks about for years.

    Marrakech & the Atlas

    Marrakech · Ourika · Atlas foothills  /  7 nights  /  8 — 12 guests

    A riad you would not find on your own, a quiet morning in the medina, an afternoon in the mountains. Hosted with a fixer we trust.

04 — Hosting

Hosting has its own return.

A trip is built around a host and the people they bring. In return, you travel, you earn, or you give it back — whichever shape fits your community.

  1. 01

    Travel without paying.

    Once your group fills the trip, your seat travels with it. No fine print, no minimum-night clause. The math is simple and the same for every host.

  2. 02

    Earn a host share.

    A clear commission on every guest you bring. Paid on a schedule you choose, in a structure that works whether you host once a year or a few times a season.

  3. 03

    Or send it forward.

    Many of our hosts redirect their share to a school, a club, a community fund — a cause their people already care about. The trip becomes a quiet way to fund it.

Dream Travel builds the trip and carries the logistics. The seats are yours to fill — your people, your audience, your circle. That is the partnership.
