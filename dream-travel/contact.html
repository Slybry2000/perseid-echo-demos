Frequently asked questions

Here are the practical details most group organizers want to know.

This page keeps the planning questions in one place so it is easier to move from interest to a real conversation.

Who are private custom group tours ideal for?

They are ideal for group leaders, creators, teachers, chefs, sports clubs, professionals, and community organizers who want to travel with their group without becoming tour operators.

What does Dream Travel take care of?

Dream Travel can handle itinerary design, accommodations, transportation, experiences, local partners, registration, payments, logistics, and on-the-ground coordination.

What group size works best?

Typical group size is 10 to 25 travelers, with private departures scheduled around season, destination availability, and what works for your audience.

What kinds of themes can we build around?

Trips can be built around art, history, language, culture, wine, culinary experiences, photography, sports, wellness, or any clear theme that gives the group a reason to travel together.
