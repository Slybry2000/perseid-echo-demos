FAQ

Questions hosts usually ask first.

These are the practical questions most hosts ask early, especially around role, structure, and what gets finalized before launch.

Do you only work with big creators or influencers?

No. A focused community with real trust and a clear trip concept can be a stronger fit than a larger but passive audience.

What role am I expected to play as the host?

You are usually the reason people join. Dream Travel can support the planning and travel backbone while you stay centered on the experience, teaching, taste, or access your audience values.

How are host benefits or compensation handled?

They are structured case by case. If host compensation, revenue share, or hosted travel is part of the model, it is defined in writing before promotion begins.

What gets finalized before the trip is promoted?

Responsibilities, pricing structure, traveler minimums, booking conditions, and the commercial structure are all confirmed before the trip goes public.
