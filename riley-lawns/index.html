INDEX / 02   WHAT WE KEEP UP

Lawns, beds, and the
rest of the property.

We keep residential grounds the way they ought to be kept. Six services that cover the cadence of a Pacific Northwest year. No subscriptions, no upsells, no surprises on the invoice.

  1. 01

    Lawn care

    Weekly or bi-weekly mow, edge, blow. Stripe pattern on request. We bag, mulch, or leave clippings depending on the season and the lawn.

  2. 02

    Cleanup & reset

    One-time service that brings an overgrown yard back to baseline. Best in early spring or after a property changes hands.

  3. 03

    Bark & mulch

    Beds refreshed each season. Dark bark for contrast, medium fir for warmth, or your spec. Delivered and spread, beds edged clean.

  4. 04

    Trimming

    Hedge shaping, shrub maintenance, light pruning. Tall ladder work for cedars and laurels when access allows.

  5. 05

    Hauling

    Yard waste, brush, blackberry, old beds. We load, haul, and dump. One-call clearance for properties that need a real reset.

  6. 06

    Power washing

    Driveways, walkways, decks, patios, fences. Soft wash for siding when needed. Pacific Northwest moss removed without surface damage.
